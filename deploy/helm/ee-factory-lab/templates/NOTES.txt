EE Factory Lab

This chart skeleton is included for teams that prefer Helm packaging over the
raw Minikube manifests. The documented local lab path uses deploy/minikube
because it keeps the platform wiring explicit for learning, review, and
portfolio demonstration.

Recommended local path:

  ./scripts/setup-minikube.ps1
  ./scripts/deploy-minikube.ps1
  ./scripts/port-forward.ps1 -StopExisting
